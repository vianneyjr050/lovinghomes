Before You Book

What to Consider

Make sure your dog has the best possible stay by keeping these in mind.

Your Dog's Temperament

Let us know if your dog is shy, energetic, or needs extra attention. We tailor care to every personality.

Vaccinations

Ensure your dog is up to date on core vaccines including rabies, distemper, and parvovirus before their stay.

Diet & Feeding Schedule

Share your dog's regular diet, portion sizes, and feeding times so we can maintain their routine.

Medications & Allergies

Inform us of any medications, supplements, or allergies so we can provide safe and proper care.

Comfort Items

Feel free to bring a familiar toy or blanket familiar scents help your dog feel right at home.

Emergency Contact

Provide a reachable contact number and your vet's details in case of any urgent situation.
